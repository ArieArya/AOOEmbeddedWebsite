YogiTrainer.

How it can help you

As more and more people look to yoga as a way to escape the hustle and bustle of every day life, YogiTrainer provides a unique and disruptive solution that brings the knowledge of yoga experts to the palms of your hands. By using our product, you can gain a deeper understanding and skill in the art wherever you may be and at your own pace. Our three main value propositions are: convenience, experience, portability, and flexibility.

Convenience

With expert advise that fits right into your pocket, you can have the full experience of your typical yoga class wherever you are, whenever you want to. With advanced features, you can constantly track your performance and gain key feedback to improve your performance.

Experience

Through the latest generation IoT and motion tracking technology, you will obtain personalized feedback on how you can improve your skill in the art. Moreover, with live audio feedback, you can be sure that you will have an expert by your side at all times to give you the best possible experience.

Portability

By integrating our platform and advanced features to a mobile device, you will have expert advise in the palm of your hands. Whether you are in the office, in the beach, or at the comfort of your own home, you will have the full yoga experience available to you at all times.

Flexibility

More than being able to bring your yoga partner wherever you deem fit, you can learn the art at your own individual pace. With expert personalized feedback and performance monitoring, you will gain the best feedback from industry experts and learn at a comfortable pace that fits your own schedule.
