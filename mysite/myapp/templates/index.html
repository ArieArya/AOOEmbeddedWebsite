YogaTech.

Introducing YogaTech, a personal IoT Yoga instructor made to help you live life to the fullest through the art of Yoga. Its state of the art technology and real-time signal processing and voice instructor will guide you from a beginner to an expert in the comfort of your own home. We believe now, more than ever, that a calm mind and spirit will guide us through these unprecedented pandemic times.

Extraordinary Performance

Powered by the latest Raspberry-Pi Zero with a 1GHz single-core CPU, YogaTech utilizes efficient multi-threading and concurrency to provide real-time signal processing and live audio feedback to you. Its powerful LIS3DH accelerometer is able to accurately trace your position and produce analytics of your performance that has been verified by industry experts. Further, by integrating our platform to mobile devices, you are able to carry the device wherever you'd like, whenever you'd like - so that you may take a break from the world around you and calm your mind. By giving you detailed feedback on your performance through the latest generation technology, you can soon leap to an expert yogi before you realize it.

Flexibility and Portability

During these unprecedented times amidst the pandemic, more and more people find themselves increasingly isolated without an opportunity to undertake supervised yoga lessons. However, it seems like now they need it more than ever. This is why YogaTech brings portability and flexibility to our device - allowing yoga to be brought wherever you are, and whenever you need it. By integrating our IoT platform with mobile devices, you are able to undertake machine-supervised yoga lessons with the highest quality feedback and performance tracking. Wherever you are, at home, in the beach, in the park, in at the office - YogaTech will always be available for you.

The Perfect Yoga Partner
